# Warranty Policy (Sample)

This sample policy is for demonstration and testing only.

## Coverage period
- Standard coverage lasts 12 months from the original purchase date.
- Extended coverage is available at purchase and adds 24 months.

## What is covered
- Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship.
- Hardware failures under normal consumer use.

## What is not covered
- Accidental damage (drops, spills, misuse, or abuse).
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications.
- Normal wear and tear (cosmetic damage, scratches, dents).
- Damage caused by power surges or environmental conditions.

## How to file a warranty claim
1) Provide proof of purchase (receipt or invoice).
2) Provide the product serial number.
3) Describe the issue and steps already taken.

## Resolution
- We will repair or replace the product at our discretion.
- If replacement is not available, a refund may be offered.

## Shipping
- Customers pay shipping to the service center.
- We cover return shipping for approved warranty claims.
